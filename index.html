About Us

Welcome to LAYA! LAYA is a Catholic young adult group based at Prince of Peace Parish in Lewiston, Maine. Our goal is to create a fertile ground to form new relationships, get involved in the local community, and grow together in our Catholic faith. While rooted in the Catholic faith, individuals from all backgrounds often attend and are always invited. New faces are welcome and our typical ages range from 18-35.

LAYA meets every Thursday and rotates between social events, service projects, and learning more about the Church’s Tradition.

If you think you might be interested in joining us, feel free to reach out through our contact page!
